What is AstraX and why visit IIT Mandi?

AstraX, the inter-college Astro-Meet of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi is the attraction center for students and professors with an inclination towards computational and theoretical astronomy.
Bringing together space science and technology, AstraX is the one and only of it's kind marked by the acing competitions, prodigious talks, lauded workshops and much more.
Surrounded by serene mountains and forests, IIT Mandi has a great night sky for astronomy enthusiasts where major constellations and the Milky Way Galaxy are visible with the naked eye. Located near the Uhl tributary, the institute has a green, pure and peaceful campus away from the city buzz.
